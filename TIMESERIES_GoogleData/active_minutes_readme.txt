Time Series Data Export

The Time Series export provides a detailed timeline of your tracked activity.

Files Included:
----------

active_minutes_YYYY-MM-DD.csv         - Where YYYY-MM-DD is the starting date for the entries in the file.

Active minutes measures active time of the user during the day.
Each entry has the following values:

    timestamp                         - Date and time at which the entry was logged.
    light                             - Minutes spent in light exercise.
    moderate                          - Minutes spent in moderate exercise.
    very                              - Minutes spent in very active exercise.
